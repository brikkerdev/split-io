## About the game (~1000)
Inkly.io is an arcade about territory capture.

The hero moves across the field and leaves a wide colored trail behind. The area enclosed by the trail becomes yours. The goal is simple: paint the entire arena down to the last pixel.

Besides the trail, the hero has an arrow. It flies straight ahead and closes a large capture on its own as soon as its path touches your territory. One precise shot — and half the map is your color.

After 100%, an upgrade card selection opens, and a new round begins. Each cycle changes the play style: the hero gets faster or holds the arrow in the air longer.

Inside you'll also find a skin collection, a daily reward, achievements and a global leaderboard.

## How to play (~1000)
Goal. Paint the entire arena your color. The more area you enclose in one run, the higher your capture percentage and the bigger the reward.

Controls. PC: mouse — direction, LMB or Space — shoot. Phone: drag your finger — the hero moves there, tap — the arrow flies.

Progress. Painted 100% — pick one of two upgrade cards and start a new round. Clear every upgrade — victory and a place on the leaderboard.

Capture. Any step beyond your zone leaves a wide colored trail behind the hero. Draw any arc with the trail and bring the hero back to your zone — the space inside instantly fills with your color, percentage and coins drop into the pot. A long smooth loop gives more than ten short ones.

Arrow. When a long run is risky — launch the arrow. It detaches from the hero and flies straight in the direction you faced at the moment of the shot. Aim the dashed line so it touches your zone again — the capture closes automatically, without a long run. There's a short cooldown between shots.
